FAQs

Answer common questions about uploading voice files and extracting audio from YouTube videos

How to upload files?

To upload a file, go to the upload page and click on the 'Choose File' button. Select the file from your device and click 'Upload' to start the process.

How to extract audio from YouTube videos?

To extract audio from a YouTube video, go to the extraction page and enter the YouTube URL link and timeline. Click 'Extract Audio' to start the extraction process.

How long does the extraction process take?

The extraction process usually takes a few seconds to a minute, depending on the length of the video.

Can I record my own vocal imitation?

Yes, you can record your own vocal imitation on the recording page. Just click on the 'Record' button and start singing or speaking.

How does the similarity analysis work?

The similarity analysis compares the recorded voice with the voice file or extracted voice. It analyzes factors such as pitch, tone, and rhythm to determine the similarity.

How accurate is the similarity analysis?

The accuracy of the similarity analysis depends on various factors, including the quality of the recording and the complexity of the voice file. It provides a general indication of similarity.

What feedback will I receive?

Based on the similarity analysis, you will receive feedback on how closely your vocal imitation matches the voice file or extracted voice. This feedback can help you improve your vocal skills.
